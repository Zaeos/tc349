My Intention and My Associates' Interpretation of my Cards

My intention with the site was more or less a "blog" format with clearly separated posts. Separate from these posts and accessible from every screen would be information about the site and myself. Each page would have styling suited to that page's content, though each would probably borrow from the rest of the pages in some ways.

Derrick's response simply split my content section into two distinct categories. This may help improve navigability and the overall flow of the site. It's an interesting idea to consider.

Jim chose to group the cards by content type, including the more miscellaneous category of Other Information in counterpoint to Dr. Who. He, like everyone else, chose to group information strictly relating to the author together with site information.

Karl's response not as complex as any of the others. Simply labeling two sections of the site as interesting and not, essentially what Karl has done, might make for an interesting design dynamic.
